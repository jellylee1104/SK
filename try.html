Your profile:

Profession: Firefighter
Age: 25
Friend: B, D, E, “Stephanie”
Relationship: in love with D

Story:

The story begins in secondary 3; you’re in the friend group with roles B, D, E and Glady (Glady is another girl who is already dead). You guys are excellent friends. However, the nightmare just began. One day you guys find out that Glady is being sad and constantly crying, but she didn’t tell you guys why, and you start to see some pain in her body. You decide to inform B and persuade B to talk to her as B and Glady have a relationship. Yet you were told that Glady is drowning on the next day in the news, and you are being guilty, blaming yourself; you should ask her when you find out the pain. The odd thing is that the news reported that when they find Glady, there is a water drop painted on her face with purple lipstick. By that time, you only think that she is being sad and has decided to suicide. You swear that you will be a person that can save people, and you become a firefighter. You and D had a crush in secondary 5 and decided to have a relationship a year later. Once, on a mission, you met Stephanie, saved her life, and you and D started being friends with her as you think she is miserable. She told us that she is studying in the same secondary school in the same year but left in secondary 3. Then, Stephanie joined a friend group including you, B and E. E and Stephanie soon have a relationship. In one gathering with D and Stephanie, when she went to the toilet and didn’t take her phone with her, you and D saw C send a message to Stephanie. The message says, “Don’t you dare threaten me; I will still pursue your brother! If you ever let him and the whole world see those videos, I will tell the whole world that you killed Glady!”. You and D are so shocked; however, you didn’t fully trust this message, you took a picture and decide to ask her later. On the next day, you know she is an accountant and went to her office. You saw her bulling F but according to what she told you she said she is the one who is being bullied. You are so angry, but you still decide to continue to pretend to be friends with Stephanie, but decide to murder her together in the gathering two weeks later as you are filled with anger. Moreover, as E is having a relationship with Stephanie, so you guys start alienating her as you guys don’t want her to suffer in pain and do the revenge yourselves, which suggests that the friend group only remain A, B and D.

On the day of gathering with the teachers in your secondary school, you and D decided to murder Stephanie by hanging her up pretend that she is suicide and that she is fear when she looks at the words on the toilet door (such as writing “YOU KILLED ME!”, “YOU DON’T DESERVE WHAT YOU HAVE NOW!”). And draw a water drop on her face using purple lipstick, just like how Glady dead.

Timeline:

The gathering starts at 17:45

16:57 – you and D arrived at the school and started playing basketball. Then you saw E & Stephanie come and go to the hall directly. Next came C and joined you to play basketball. After a while, F came and went to the hall; finally came B and she joined the basketball team to play.

17:45 – The gathering starts and all of you go to the hall to watch the performance.

19:00 – End performance and a break for students and teachers to chat. You walked with D to find teachers.

19:12 – You separated from D and went to the toilet yourself to prepare for the murder. You take out the rope you prepared and find a space to hang it up; you put it in the ceiling of the last toilet compartment. At around 19:15, you did this preparation and ran into B in front of the toilet.

19:16 – You find D and continue to talk to teachers together.

19:21 - You separate from D again as she says she wants to go to the toilet; you start wondering and after around 2 minutes, hear Stephanie tell E that she is not feeling well and she is going to the toilet, knowing that she is going by herself and she knows this may be one opportunity. However, you decided to wait for D.

19:25 – D returned and told you that it will be obvious if she goes to the toilet again now, and you guys decided to stick to your original plan.

19:35 – You guys find out that Stephanie is still in the toilet and you decide to kill her now. When you entered the toilet, you saw her lying on the toilet seat with her eyes closed. However, D still stuns Stephanie with the stun spray and you guys hang her up by the rope together. You have completed the murder. While D pull out all the liquid in the stun spray and filled with water, you write words on the toilet door. (“YOU KILLED ME!”, “YOU DON’T DESERVE WHAT YOU HAVE NOW!”) and draw a water drop on her face using purple lipstick. You then throw the whiteboard pen and the lipstick into the bin.

19:37 – You guys went out of the toilet and found a teacher to chat. In the meantime, you saw F go into the toilet; however, she went out so quickly and didn’t scream or say that she saw Stephanie hanging up. Instead, she pretends nothing has happened.

19:45 – Dinner time starts, you and D go back to the hall and notice that everyone except Stephanie arrived already.

19:50 – Stephanie is still not here and E said she hasn’t found her since 19:25 and said she might not feel well. We all go and find her, and finally find that she is hung up in the toilet with words on the door.
